Best Movies According to Hoor

My favorite top 3 movies

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Directed by Frank Darabont, this film tells the story of a man who is wrongfully imprisoned and forms a deep bond with a fellow inmate while trying to maintain his hope and dignity.

The Godfather (1972)

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this classic film follows the Corleone family, a powerful Italian-American mafia clan, as they navigate loyalty, power struggles, and crime in post-World War II America.

The Dark Knight (2008)

Directed by Christopher Nolan, this superhero film explores the complex relationship between Batman and the Joker as they engage in a battle of ideologies in Gotham City. The film is known for Heath Ledger's iconic portrayal of the Joker.
